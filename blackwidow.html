Synopsis
In Marvel Studios’ action-packed spy thriller “Black Widow,” Natasha Romanoff
aka Black Widow confronts the darker parts of her ledger when a dangerous conspiracy with
ties to her past arises. Pursued by a force that will stop at nothing
to bring her down, Natasha must deal with her history as a spy and the broken
relationships left in her wake long before she became an Avenger. Scarlett Johansson
reprises her role as Natasha/Black Widow
